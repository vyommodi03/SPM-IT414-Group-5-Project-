Faq's

Q?

How soon will my order reach me?

A.

Depending on your location, your order should reach you within 7 working days.

Q?

Is there an additional cost for shipping?

A.

No, we provide free shipping on all domestic orders.

Q?

What is the security deposit?

A.

Every time you reserve an outfit you will be asked to pay a security deposit that is equal to the rental price of your outfit. For example, if your outfit costs ₹5,000, we will add a security deposit of ₹5,000 to your bill, making the total ₹10,000. Since all our garments are authentic designer pieces, the security deposit is just standard protocol and serves as an insurance cover for our outfits whilst they are with you.

Q?

What is the condition of the garment?

A.

These garments are pre-owned designer wear clothes sourced from our reliable network of fashionistas or never worn sample pieces sourced directly from the designers. We have categorised the garments under 4 types: New, As Good As New, Gently used and Adequately Used.

Q?

Can I pay for the garment in cash?

A.

Sorry, but all payments can only be made online.
